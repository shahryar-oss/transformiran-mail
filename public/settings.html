Recipient privacy is a feature, not a setting.

TI Mail does not track when recipients open your emails or click your links. Other apps make this a toggle you have to turn off — we just don't build it.

Sending

Window where you can hit Undo after sending. Longer = safer; shorter = snappier.

When sending, type-shortcuts become formatting: **bold**, *italic*, `code`, ~~strike~~, [text](url), # heading, lists with - or 1.. Off by default. Quoted replies are left untouched.

Default meeting link

When you ask Delta to create a meeting, it'll attach this link to the event. Also auto-fills into the Calendar event form when you create one by hand.

Just for display — Delta attaches whatever URL you paste below regardless of provider.

Use your permanent meeting room link so every event Delta creates can be joined from the same URL. How to find your permanent Zoom link →

People you schedule for

If you book meetings for someone as their assistant, list their email here. When a meeting you set up includes them, Delta treats them as the host: it attaches their saved meeting link (not yours) and leaves you off the invite — you're arranging it between them and the other person. One email per line.

For the meeting to land on their calendar (and stay off yours entirely), they should share their Google Calendar with you and grant "Make changes to events". Otherwise Delta still keeps you off the invite, but the event is organized from your calendar.
